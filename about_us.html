About Us

Our Mission

The Society of St. Vincent de Paul Holy Angels Conference was formed in March 2001. Our society is one of five conferences in the immediate Aurora area, who divide geographically to assist those in need. Holy Angels serves those on the south and west side of Aurora, Illinois. All Vincentians are volunteers.

No work of charity is foreign to the Society. Vincentians provide person to person assistance that promote human dignity and integrity. The Society is concerned not only with relieving need but also with addressing the situation that causes it. It serves persons in need regardless of creed, opinion, color, cast or origin.
